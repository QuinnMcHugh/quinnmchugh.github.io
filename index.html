About

Hi! My name is Quinn, and I am a freshman Computer Science student at The Ohio State University. I have been programming since my sophomore year of high school, and have been hooked ever since. Along the way, I have picked up skills like C++, Java, and even Assembly, but nothing excites me more than the thrill of a challenging problem. As somebody who enjoys a good challenge, I naturally joined the Ohio State chapter of the ACM, where the best-of-the-best go to hone their algorithm skills. Looking forward, I plan to change what society believes to be possible by harnessing the true potential of machines. If you would like to reach out to me, or offer me a job, visit my contact page.
